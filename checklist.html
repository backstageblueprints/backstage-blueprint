// WHY THESE TEN

Why these ten.

These ten are the ones that — when they fail — take the show down with them. They cover the full lifecycle of a tour, from the first conversation about what the artist wants to build, to the last verification before the truck pulls away from the dock.

Use them as a working reference. Print them and stick them in your binder. Run a show through them on paper before the truck rolls. Hand them to a new crew lead so they understand what gets confirmed at every stage.
